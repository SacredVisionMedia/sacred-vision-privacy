Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 29, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 29, 2026

Sacred Vision ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Sacred Vision Poster application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our App.

1. What the App Does

The Sacred Vision Poster App is a private, internal publishing tool used solely to automate the posting of Sacred Vision content — including video links and images — to Pinterest. The App is not available to the general public and does not collect data from end users.

2. Information We Access

To publish content on Pinterest, the App uses OAuth 2.0 to authenticate with Pinterest on behalf of the Sacred Vision business account. This grants the App limited access to:

  • Create and manage Pins on the Sacred Vision Pinterest account
  • Read basic account information (username, board list)

We do not access, store, or share any personal data from Pinterest users or followers.

3. Data Storage

Access tokens and credentials are stored locally on a private, password-protected machine and are never transmitted to third parties. No user data is stored in any external database.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties. The App interacts only with the Pinterest API on behalf of our own account.

5. Third-Party Services

This App communicates with the Pinterest API v5. Your use of Pinterest is governed by Pinterest's Privacy Policy.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
