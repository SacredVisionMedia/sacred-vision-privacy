Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 23, 2026  |  Last Updated: June 26, 2026

Sacred Vision ("we," "our," or "us") operates private, internal publishing tools used to manage and automate Sacred Vision content across social media platforms including Pinterest, TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, and Instagram (the "Apps"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when these Apps interact with third-party platform APIs.

1. What the Apps Do

The Sacred Vision Apps are private, internal publishing tools used solely to automate the posting and management of Sacred Vision content — including video links, images, and captions — to Sacred Vision-owned accounts on supported platforms. The Apps are not available to the general public and do not collect data from end users.

2. Information We Access

To publish content on supported platforms, the Apps use OAuth 2.0 to authenticate with each platform on behalf of the Sacred Vision business account. This grants limited access to:

  • Create and manage posts, Pins, Reels, and Shorts on Sacred Vision accounts
  • Read basic account information (username, board list, channel info)
  • Read engagement metrics (views, likes, comments) on Sacred Vision content
  • Verify the authorized Sacred Vision account on each platform

We do not access, store, or share any personal data from platform users or followers.

3. Data Storage

Access tokens and credentials are stored locally on a private, password-protected machine and are never transmitted to third parties. No user data is stored in any external database.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties. The Apps interact only with each platform's official API on behalf of our own Sacred Vision accounts.

5. What We Do Not Do

The Apps do not:

  • Sell or share personal information
  • Automate engagement such as likes, comments, follows, or saves
  • Access or post to accounts not owned or administered by Sacred Vision
  • Scrape any platform
  • Run advertising or manage catalogs

6. Third-Party Services

These Apps communicate with the following third-party APIs:

  • Pinterest API v5
  • TikTok API
  • YouTube Data API v3
  • Meta Graph API (Facebook & Instagram)

Your use of each platform is governed by their respective privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

These Apps are not directed at children under 13 and do not knowingly collect information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
